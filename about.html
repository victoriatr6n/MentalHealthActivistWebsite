Hello, I'm Victoria! I created Gains for Brains as part of the Girls Who Code Summer Self-Paced Program. The main purpose of Gains for Brains is to highlight the importance of mental health. I want to emphasize that your mental health is just as important as your physical health, so you should be taking care of both! I hope this website allows you to learn more about the current issues with mental health and ways you can support yourself and others who are struggling with their mental health.
